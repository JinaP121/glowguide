Daily Skincare

AM Cleansing (Morning Routine)

For healthy skin:
Use a mild acidic cleanser, enzyme cleanser, or just rinse with water.

For sensitive or weak skin barrier:
• Water only: Protects barrier but may not fully remove dirt.
• Cleanser: Cleans well but may weaken the barrier.

Recommendation:
Skip heavy products at night & cleanse with water or an enzyme cleanser in the morning.

PM Cleansing (Night Routine)

Water alone is not enough!

Use a cleanser to remove dirt, oil, and makeup.

If you wear makeup or sunscreen → Double cleansing is necessary!

  1. Cleansing Oil/Cream: Gently massage for 30 sec – 1 min, then rinse with lukewarm water.
  2. Foam Cleanser: Lather, cleanse the entire face (focus on T-zone & U-zone), and rinse thoroughly.

Midday Cleansing

If your face feels sticky or dirty, rinse with water and apply moisturizer.

Weekly Extras

Lifestyle Habits for Healthy Skin

• Exercise, stress management, healthy diet, enough sleep
(Aim to sleep by 10–11 PM, at the latest by 12 AM).

Note on Sensitive Skin

• Be cautious with paper sheet masks.
• If you have sensitive skin, avoid using masks until your skin barrier is restored.
• Start with a regenerating cream first, then introduce hydrating masks.

Moisturizing & Skin Barrier Care

• Using a moisturizer is essential to maintain healthy skin.
• Avoid harsh exfoliation; use hydrating products that support the skin barrier.

Hydration & Sheet Mask Tips

• Keep skin hydrated at all times.
• Using a sheet mask daily is okay, but avoid leaving it on for more than 10–15 minutes.
• Check ingredients (look for Hydrogen).

Recommended Masks

• Hydrating masks
• NO Whitening & anti-wrinkle masks (may cause irritation with frequent use).
• Use functional masks twice a week max.
