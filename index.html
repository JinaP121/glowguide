What is Glow Guide?

Glow Guide is about achieving naturally healthy, glowing skin not just surface shine. This website helps you build good skincare habits with simple routines and lifestyle tips that anyone can follow. No expensive products are needed, and there are no ads or brand promotions just real, effective skincare methods. Healthy skin comes from daily habits. Let’s create better skincare routines together.

Q&A:

Q: Why is the morning and night skincare routine different?

A: In the morning, sun protection and light hydration are important. At night, you need to remove dirt and help your skin recover.

Q: My skin feels tight after washing. Is that normal?

A: No! If your skin feels tight, it may mean your skin barrier is weak or your cleanser is too harsh. Try using a gentle cleanser.

Q: How many times should I wash my face a day?

A: Twice a day (morning and night) is best. Washing too often can damage your skin barrier.

Q: Do I need to use a moisturizer?

A: Yes! No matter your skin type, moisturizer is important. Even oily skin needs hydration.

Q: Do I really need to wear sunscreen?

A: Yes! UV rays can reach your skin even indoors, so sunscreen is a must every day. If you stay inside, SPF 30 is usually enough.
